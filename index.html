Our signature · fresh-baked tortillas

The one thing no one else in Sydney does.

We're your whole Mexican pantry — but this is what makes us different. Supermarket tortillas were baked weeks ago, in a factory, far away. Ours are fired this morning, on the comal, right here in North Rocks.

01

The masa

Real corn masa — the foundation of a true tortilla, not flour and fillers.

02

The comal

Onto the hot comal it goes. Heat is the craft — this is where Fireworks earns its name.

03

The puff

It puffs, it chars, it steams. Soft, warm, alive — the way a tortilla is meant to be.

04

In your hands

Baked, not shipped. From the comal to your table the same day — gluten-free.
